    Creating a Recipie(Chapathi)

    • Ingredients
      • Wheat Flour
      • Water
      • Salt
      • Oil
    • Instructions
      1. Mix flour, water, salt to form dough.
      2. Divide dough into small balls.
      3. Roll each ball into a flat circle.
      4. Heat a pan and cook each chapathi until golden brown on both sides.
      5. Serve hot with curry or chutney.
